# Dashboard Notes



This document describes the planned Power BI dashboard for the Loan Portfolio Risk Analytics project.



## Dashboard Purpose



The dashboard is designed to help users monitor loan applications, approval patterns, credit risk, and exposure trends.



## Dashboard Layout



### 1. Executive Summary



Top-level KPI cards:



- Total Applications

- Total Requested Loan Amount

- Average Loan Amount

- Average Credit Score

- Approval Rate

- High-Risk Applications



### 2. Risk Overview



Visuals:



- Applications by Credit Risk Band

- Approval Rate by Risk Band

- High-Risk vs Low-Risk Applications



### 3. Applicant Segmentation



Visuals:



- Approval Rate by Employment Type

- Average Loan Amount by Education

- Applications by City

- Average Credit Score by Gender



### 4. Exposure Analysis



Visuals:



- Loan-to-Income Ratio

- High Exposure Applications

- Loan Amount Distribution



### 5. Drill-Down Table



Fields:



- Age

- Income

- LoanAmount

- CreditScore

- YearsExperience

- Gender

- Education

- City

- EmploymentType

- LoanApproved



## Planned Filters



- City

- Gender

- Education

- EmploymentType

- Credit Risk Band

- Loan Approval Status



## Intended Users



- Business analysts

- Risk analysts

- Reporting teams

- Portfolio monitoring teams
